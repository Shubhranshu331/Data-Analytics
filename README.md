### 1. 🚴 Bike Sales Performance Dashboard
**Tool:** Tableau &nbsp;|&nbsp; **Domain:** Retail & Sales Analytics

An interactive dashboard visualizing global bike sales trends across customer demographics, product categories, and regional markets. Analyzes key financial metrics including revenue, profit margins, and unit costs to identify top-performing products and customer segments.

**Key Insights:**
- Identified highest revenue-generating product categories and regions
- Analyzed customer demographics to uncover purchase behavior patterns
- Enabled data-driven decisions for inventory and sales strategy optimization

### 2. 🏢 Classic Models Sales Dashboard
**Tool:** Power BI &nbsp;|&nbsp; **Domain:** Business Performance Analytics

An interactive sales performance and profitability analysis using transactional data from a classic model cars company. Focuses on business KPIs including revenue margins, product line popularity, and geographic sales distribution.

**Key Insights:**

- Mapped geographic sales distribution to identify high-value markets

### 3. ⚡ Washington EV Population Dashboard
**Tool:** Tableau &nbsp;|&nbsp; **Domain:** Sustainability & Transportation Analytics

An interactive dashboard visualizing electric vehicle adoption trends across Washington State. Analyzes vehicle make/model distribution, battery range, MSRP, and geographic spread to provide insights into EV infrastructure requirements and consumer preferences.

**Key Insights:**
- Mapped EV concentration across counties to identify infrastructure gaps
- Analyzed battery range vs. MSRP to understand consumer preferences
- Tracked year-on-year EV adoption growth to forecast future demand

**🔗 Live Interactive Dashboard:** [Open in Tableau Public](https://public.tableau.com/app/profile/shubhranshu.shubhranshu/viz/WashingtonEVDashboard_17795234433300/Dashboard1)

### 4. 🎬 Netflix Content Analysis Dashboard
**Tool:** Power BI &nbsp;|&nbsp; **Domain:** Media & Entertainment Analytics

A comprehensive look at Netflix's content library tracking content growth, genre popularity, and geographic production trends. Uses a relational data model linking titles, cast, directors, and countries to enable deep-dive filtering into content strategy.

**Key Insights:**
- Tracked year-on-year content growth across Movies vs. TV Shows
- Identified top-producing countries and most popular genres
- Analyzed director and cast networks to understand content strategy

**▶️ Live Demo:** [Watch Dashboard Walkthrough](https://youtu.be/R5TZUIijL1Q)
